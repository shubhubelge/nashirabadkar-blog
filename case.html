Website Design

Enphase Energy is a solar power leader in the
international market, known for it’s powerful microinverter
panel and full functioning system.

Creative Goal

Enphase is well-known on the global front. However, in India due to lack of category awareness people have not yet explored the idea of how renewable energy can help the common man in their day to day lives.

Creative Goal

Looking at the core market, we first identified every regional challenge for consumers regarding electricity & it’s availability.

We designed a series of awareness creatives which build our brand awareness. Following it up with content unique to each region, we addressed TG-specific benefits of Enphase products. This strategy has enabled the brand to grow from a small unit to showcasing at most of India Energy Expo & even launching battery back-up in the Indian market.
